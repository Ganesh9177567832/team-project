Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I search for a movie?

A: Use the search bar on the homepage to enter the movie name and get results instantly.

Q2: Can I save movies to watch later?

A: Yes! Click the "Add to Watchlist" button under any movie to save it for later viewing.

Q3: Do I need to create an account to use this app?

A: Yes, you need to register and login to access watchlist and profile features.

Q4: Can I edit my profile details?

A: Yes! Just go to the Profile page and click on "Edit Profile" to update your information.

Q5: How do I logout from the app?

A: Click on the "Logout" option in the navbar or profile page to logout safely.
